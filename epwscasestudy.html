Exterior Power Washing Solutions

The Project

Exterior Power Washing Solutions is a business website that needed a makeover. The client felt that the website did not reflect the look of the company. I set out to create a website that was simple, navigable, and would increase new business inquiries.

The Old Website

Since I did not have access to current website data, I started out by wireframing the current homepage as well as creating a sitemap with all the current content. I concluded that the main issues were as follows:

  • Unclear hierarchy since links to other pages were scattered across the homepage.
  • The picture in the hero area did not reflect the business.
  • Calls-to-action were not distinguishable between the other links on the page
  • Important information was not readily available/could be consolidated from other pages
