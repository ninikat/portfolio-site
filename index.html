About Me

Hello, my name is Nydia Segura and I am a full-stack developer and UX Designer. My professional background includes Software QA and a degree from the University of Houston in Computer Engineering Technology. Testing and searching for bugs in software led me to not only want to build it myself but also to learn how to optimize features and design for better user experiences! I live in Houston, TX and when I am not following tutorials online, I enjoy running, baking, hanging out with my dogs, and making elaborate chalkboard art for my friend's special occasions!
